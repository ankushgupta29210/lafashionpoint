LaFashionPoint – Images Folder
================================

Add your product photos and logo here.

LOGO
----
- Filename: logo.png  (recommended: 300x80 px, transparent background)
- Then in index.html, replace the text logo in the <nav> and footer with:
  <img src="images/logo.png" alt="LaFashionPoint" height="44">

PRODUCT IMAGES
--------------
Add one photo per category and update the CSS in css/style.css.
Look for comments like /* bg-tshirt */ and replace the gradient with:
  background: url('../images/tshirt.jpg') center/cover no-repeat;

Recommended image sizes: 600x500 px (landscape)

Files to add:
  tshirt.jpg
  shirt.jpg
  hoodie.jpg
  jeans.jpg
